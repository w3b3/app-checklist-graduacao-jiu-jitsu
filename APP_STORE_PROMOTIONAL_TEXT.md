1. **Lead with the problem, NOT the solution**
   - ‚ùå "App com checklist de Jiu-Jitsu"
   - ‚úÖ "Esqueceu qual t√©cnica falta?"

2. **Use academy name for trust**
   - Always include "Brothers Fight" for credibility
   - Students trust academy-endorsed tools

3. **Keep it conversational, not corporate**
   - Use "voc√™", "seu/sua" (you, your)
   - Avoid jargon: "app", "requisitos", "progresso" are OK
